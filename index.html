    RAM MANDIR


    Ram Mandir is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ram. It is inaugurated on 22 January,2024 and located in Ayodhya(Janambhoomi),Uttar pardesh,India.The temple has beautiful architecture with carved sculptures.

    RAM LALLA


    In 1949, the idols of Ram Lalla were surreptitiously placed inside the Babri Masjid, leading to further escalation of tensions. In 1986, a district court in India ordered the opening of the site for Hindu worshippers, citing their rights to perform religious ceremonies.
